Services

24/7 Emergency Care

A 24/7 emergency hospital service, also known as an emergency department or ER, provides immediate medical care for urgent and potentially life-threatening conditions, available around the clock, every day of the week.

Laboratory Services

Hospital laboratory services, also known as clinical or medical laboratories, play a crucial role in healthcare by providing diagnostic, monitoring, and treatment-related information through various tests and analyses on patient samples.

Pharmacy Services

Hospital pharmacy services encompass the entire medication lifecycle, from procurement and storage to dispensing, compounding, and providing drug information to patients and healthcare professionals, all aimed at optimizing patient outcomes.

Cardiology

A hospital's cardiology department specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart-related conditions, offering services like consultations, diagnostic tests (ECG, echocardiograms), and treatments (medications, angioplasty).

Neurology

A hospital's neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves, addressing conditions like stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis.

Pediatrics

A hospital's pediatric department, or Paediatrics department, provides comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, encompassing routine checkups, vaccinations, and treatment of various illnesses and conditions.

Eye Care

In a hospital setting, the eye care department, also known as the Ophthalmology department, focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders, including performing surgeries and prescribing corrective lenses.
